Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 12, 2026

Overview

This custom GPT ("PokeAPI Assistant") is a free tool that answers Pokemon-related questions by querying the public PokeAPI (pokeapi.co). It does not collect, store, or sell any personal data.

Data Collection

This application does not collect, store, or process any personal information. Your conversations are handled entirely by OpenAI's ChatGPT platform and are subject to OpenAI's Privacy Policy.

Third-Party Services

This GPT makes API requests to PokeAPI (pokeapi.co), a free and open RESTful API. These requests contain only Pokemon-related query parameters (such as a Pokemon name or ID). No user-identifying information is sent to PokeAPI. PokeAPI's terms are available at pokeapi.co.

Cookies and Tracking

This application does not use cookies, analytics, or any tracking mechanisms.

Children's Privacy

This application does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated occasionally. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can reach out via the GPT's profile page on ChatGPT.
